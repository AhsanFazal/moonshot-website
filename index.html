WHY?
It’s simple. Over time the price of bitcoin rises. Over time the price of space travel falls. Thus, if we put a wallet on the moon anyone can contribute to the bounty and trigger a race to the moon for the bounty. Simple, right?

OKAY, BUT HOW?
It's 2018. There's a roadster on it's way to mars. Countries are using magic internet money. Pundits are discussing politician's tweets on national television. 20k flamethrowers were sold as a spontaneous joke. The future is now. It's time to take bitcoin to the moon. Literally.
STEP 1 BUILDING THE ECOSYSTEM
DESCRIPTION
Goal: rally enthusiasm and recruit the needed muscle and knowledge expertise to approach this large problem. We need a wide group of varied skills and aggregate their efforts into a coordinated effort.
GOAL
We start building the neccessary community of experts and hustlers to pull this off. The focus of community building is finding the right skill sets to overcome the bottlenecks of placing a privatekey on a rocket going to the moon. As we establish the vision with the founding team, we clarify our goals and objectives for the build phase.
STEP 2 BUILD PHASE
DESCRIPTION
Goal: defining the problems with clear definitions of dones, and collecting the proposals to solve these problems.
GOAL
Time to get cracking! There's a few moving parts to pull this off. First we break down the problems into specific challenges and clarify the definition of done. We then solicit these challenges to the community to collect various proposals for how to approach each problem.
STEP 3 CROWDFUND
DESCRIPTION
Goal: organizing the proposals into a clear strategy, defining the costs, designing the crowdfund (+ incentives), raising the money.
GOAL
Once we have clear proposals for each problem, the goal is organizing all solutions into a single strategy, defining the costs, and raising the funds needed to pay for the launch materials.
